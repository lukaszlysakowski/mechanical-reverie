Each piece is not an illustration of the tweets but a translation: the day's themes, the images' color temperature, the emotional register of the language — encoded into a genome that governs palette, pixel density, flow, geometry, and mechanical presence. Candidates render; the best is selected by an aesthetic fitness function, with curatorial overrides when theme and fitness disagree. Winners breed future generations.

Recent chapters in the engine's own evolution — a disorder dial (order collapsing along a spatial gradient, after Molnár and Nees), canonical seeds (each artwork a reproducible score, not just a print), and a sonic dimension (every genome already encodes a chord — press ◐ Sound in the generator to hear it).
